## 2. Religious content disclaimer

Rahma is a software tool. It is **not** a substitute for a qualified
scholar.

- Prayer times are calculated locally with the `adhan` package and
  parameters of your choice (calculation method + madhab). Calibration
  may differ from your local mosque's posted times. **Treat the app's
  times as guidance; if your local mosque's published schedule differs,
  defer to the local mosque.**
- The Qibla bearing is computed from your GPS location to the Kaaba's
  coordinates. The compass dial uses the device's magnetic compass and
  may have a few degrees of error. For personal prayer at home, this is
  fine; for building orientation or land surveying, use a calibrated
  instrument.
- The Quran text is bundled from the public-domain Tanzil Uthmani
  edition with the Sahih International English translation. We do not
  claim any new authority over either.
- The "Islamic Knowledge" search returns answers from a small curated
  pool with explicit source citations to Sahih hadith and Quran verses.
  These are **general guidance, not a fatwa**. For personal rulings —
  especially in matters of marriage, divorce, finance, or any case of
  doubt — consult a qualified local scholar.
